# Risk Indicators (Reference)

## Financial Statement Red Flags
- Declining revenue for 2+ consecutive years without documented restructuring.
- Negative operating cash flow with rising receivables (revenue quality concern).
- Debt/EBITDA >4x or interest coverage <1.5x.
- Qualified audit opinion or emphasis of matter on going concern.
- Related-party transactions >15% of revenue or purchases without arm's-length disclosure.

## Bank Conduct Red Flags
- Round-tripping patterns (same counterparty credit/debit cycles).
- Frequent inward cheque returns.
- Owner drawings exceeding 30% of net cash generation.
- Loan repayments to unknown NBFCs/lenders not in bureau.

## Bureau Red Flags
- Experian/CIBIL score below 650 for commercial facilities.
- 30+ DPD in last 12 months on any trade line.
- High lender concentration with deteriorating utilization.
- Guarantor exposure disproportionate to stated business scale.

## GST / Statutory Red Flags
- Filing gaps >2 months in last 12 months.
- GST liability payment ratio <80%.
- Material gap between GST outward and FS/bank turnover.

## Qualitative Triggers
- Management unwillingness to provide debt schedule or related-party list.
- Recent change in auditors or delayed financials.
- Pending litigation not disclosed in notes.
