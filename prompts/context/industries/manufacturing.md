# Manufacturing Sector Overlay

- Typical gross margin: 15-25%; EBITDA margin: 8-15%.
- Working capital: inventory 45-90 days, receivables 45-75 days acceptable.
- Debt/EBITDA tolerance: up to 3.5x for established units with order visibility.
- Key probes: capacity utilization, raw material price pass-through, export dependence.
